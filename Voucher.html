ALL PACKS
The course lasts 4 hours.
You will have an instructor with you for the entire duration.
All equipment, such as a helmet and climbing shoes, is provided.
Each pack has a different difficulty, but all climbs take place on the same mountain.

1. Beginner 10$
This is the easiest level.
It's intended for people with no climbing experience.
You will learn the basic skills of climbing and how to handle the equipment.
The highest difficulty rating will be '4'.
2. Intermediate 15$
This pack is for people who have climbed before.
After a short introduction, you will start climbing at a higher level and learn new techniques.
The highest difficulty rating will be '6'.
3. Expert 20$
This pack is for experienced climbers who want to push their limits.
After stretching, you will climb overhangs and challenging sections, depending on your choice.
The highest difficulty rating will be '8+'.
